Project Conventions

Naming convention (function names/variables/file naming):
-all lower-case
-words separated by "_"
HTML:
-all lower-case
-words separated by "-"

Controller method names:
-index (listing of all instances of a model)
-create (post method)
-new (opens form for object creation)
-edit
-show (shows page of one ojbect)
-update
-destroy
